Solutions for Farmer

Vaidik Agri Producer Company Limited is having the strength of more than 1200 farmers with the land capacity of approx 1500 acre which enables it to produce Quantity, and the vaidik team gives it quality by technical guidance. The availability of farm produce is significant factors that have a direct impact on operations of downstream of agro and food based industries. Using Data of land farm production, Agricultural Science and Machine technologies, the company has the almost accurate projection of production and post harvesting facilites. So it can meet the bulk requirement of the food processing companies. This mechanism that helps our institutional buyers better plan their operations.
